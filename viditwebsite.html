I play Sports

I am not only an artist, but I am also an athlete. I spend a lot of time on my two favorite sports, Tennis and Soccer. I love playing both sports along with doing art.

I do coding

I also spend some of my time programming. I build games and websites only to later edit them and make it fancier or better. I love python and HTML specially.

I am an art company!

I have been drawing for multiple years, and recently I have started becoming much better. I then started thinking if they was a way to monetize my talent of art. Then I thought up the idea to make a buisness where I make drawings costimized by you guys. I had anyway been wanted to make a website for a long time, so here I am.

I was going to take on the classic ways of making an art company. I would make drawings and then post them on my website for a certain price. But then I thought why waste my time making drawings that might not even get sold and instead ask you to upload pictures that you want me to draw. Then I will only draw pictures that I know will sell, and it will be customized just the way you want it.

The idea of being different from others was one I immeadiately loved. Then I took that idea and implemented it to make this buisness I currently have,
